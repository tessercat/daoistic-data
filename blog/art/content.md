# All the World's a Stage

大道汎兮其可左右
Dao is temperamental. It could go either way.

萬物恃之而生而不辭功成不名有
Every one of us has a role to play,
but it casts us and doesn't explain it to us.
We're skilled artists who don't follow a script.
衣養萬物而不為主常無欲可名於小
It costumes and coaches us,
but it doesn't control us.
Looking at it objectively,
you could say it's insignificant.
萬物歸焉而不為主可名為大
We all respond to it
but we don't influence it.
You could say it's great.

以其終不自為大故能成其大
In the end,
we're not trying to be great,
and that's what lets us do great work.
