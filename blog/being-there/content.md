# Being There

The art of walking is in leaving no trace.
The art of talking is in finding no fault.
The art of design isn't in a textbook.
The art of unassailable defense is in barring no gate.
The art of unbreakable friendship is in swearing no oath.
善行無轍迹
善言無瑕讁
善數不用籌策
善閉無關楗而不可開
善結無繩約而不可解

So of course wise people
are always willing to help people so that no one gets left behind,
and they're always happy to fix things so that nothing stays broken.
They call it perennial wisdom.
是以聖人
常善救人故無棄人
常善救物故無棄物
是謂襲明

It follows that
healthy human beings are experts at helping unhealthy human beings,
and people in need are the good person's calling.
Ignoring the experts
and avoiding the problem,
even for a genius,
is a big mistake.
They call that a vital clue.
故善人者不善人之師
不善人者善人之資
不貴其師
不愛其資
雖智大迷
是謂要妙
