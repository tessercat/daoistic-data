# Death and Taxes

We live in a small town.
We have a few neighbours.
小國寡民

We have modern technology
but no one uses it.
使有什伯之器而不用

We elect someone who mourns the dead
and no one ever moves away.
使民重死而不遠徙

Even if we had ships and wagons,
why would anyone leave?
雖有舟輿無所乘之

Even if we had an army,
what would we be fighting for?
雖有甲兵無所陳之

We elect someone who collects taxes
and we use it well.
使民復結繩而用之

Our food is delicious,
our clothing is beautiful,
our homes are peaceful,
our ways are friendly.
甘其食
美其服
安其居
樂其俗

Our homesteads are within sight of each other.
Our chickens and dogs can hear one another.
Neighbours grow old and die without annoying each other.
鄰國相望
雞犬之聲相聞
民至老死不相往來
