# Ironic

Good students hear about Dao and work hard, but they follow it.
Average students hear about Dao and some find it, some are lost.
Bad students hear about Dao and make fun of it.
If it wasn't so ridiculous, it wouldn't be worth thinking of as Dao,
so they offer these tips to help us get the hang of it.
上士聞道勤而行之
中士聞道若存若亡
下士聞道大笑之
不笑不足以為道
故建言有之

Enlightened Dao seems ignorant,
progressive Dao seems backwards,
balanced Dao seems stubborn.
明道若昧
進道若退
夷道若纇

The highest De seems crude,
the greatest sincerity seems rude.
上德若谷
太白若辱

Generous De seems stingy,
normal De seems lazy,
absolute truth might be fickle.
廣德若不足
建德若偷
質真若渝

It's a great big square without any corners.
It's a big heavy pot that holds nothing inside.
It's a big booming voice that hardly anyone hears.
It's a grand unified theory without any proof.
大方無隅
大器晚成
大音希聲
大象無形

Dao is shy and gets no respect,
but above all Dao is good at forgive and forget.
道隱無名
夫唯道善貸且成
