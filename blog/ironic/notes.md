This poem makes the most sense to me
as tips from 下士
to 上士 and 中士
on what 道 and 德 look like
when you 大笑 at 
the highfalutin language
that true believers use
to describe them. 

谷 is a valley,
but based on a footnote to this poem
in Scott Cook's _Guodian_,
I take it uncritically as 俗,
which makes a lot more sense to me than 谷
in opposition to 上.

Otherwise I'm fairly sure that
my word choices are at least arguable,
if not conventional.
