# Power

There was something whole and complete
before heaven and earth were born.
Still and silent,
eternal and timeless,
moving in endless cycles,
I think of it as the all-mother,
but I don't know what to call it.
有物混成
先天地生
寂兮寥兮
獨立不改
周行而不殆
可以為天下母
吾不知其名

I write 道
and believers say it's great,
but greatness means decline,
decline means division,
and division means strife.
字之曰道
強為之名曰大
大曰逝
逝曰遠
遠曰反

When 道 is great,
the heavens are majestic,
the earth is sacred,
and leaders are supreme.
All four glorify the motherland,
but leaders were meant to unite us,
and we are to the earth,
as earth is to the heavens,
as the heavens are to 道,
as 道 is to what is.
故道大
天大
地大
王亦大
域中有四大
而王居其一焉
人法地
地法天
天法道
道法自然
