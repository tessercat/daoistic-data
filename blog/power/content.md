# Power

There was something whole and complete
before heaven and earth were born.
Still and silent,
eternal and timeless,
moving in endless cycles,
I think of it as the all-mother,
but I don't know what to call it.
有物混成
先天地生
寂兮寥兮
獨立不改
周行而不殆
可以為天下母
吾不知其名

In a word, it's 道,
and if you must, call it great
because greatness recedes,
recedes and retires,
retires and returns.
字之曰道
強為之名曰大
大曰逝
逝曰遠
遠曰反

Since 道 is great,
the heavens are majestic,
the earth is sacred,
and sovereigns are powerful too.
All four glorify the motherland,
but sovereignty has a place in just one,
and people are to the earth,
as earth is to the heavens,
as the heavens are to 道,
as 道 is to what is.
故道大
天大
地大
王亦大
域中有四大
而王居其一焉
人法地
地法天
天法道
道法自然
