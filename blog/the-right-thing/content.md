# The Right Thing

載營魄抱一 能無離乎
Hop on the soul train and hold on. Don't let yourself get left behind.

專氣致柔 能嬰兒乎
Gather your energy and soften. Let yourself become a newborn child.

滌除玄覽 能無疵乎
Wash away your darkest thoughts. Don't let yourself cry.

愛民治國 能無知乎
Nurture your relationships and solve common problems. Don't let yourself judge.

天門開闔 能為雌乎
The universe shifts and slides. Let yourself take it all in.

明白四達 能無知乎
Bright light in every direction. Don't let yourself think.

生之 畜之 生而不有 為而不恃 長而不宰
Create something and shepherd it.
Create, but don't own it.
Act, but don't force it.
Lead, but don't control it.

是謂玄德
This one's called Xuande,
the strange case of doing the right thing.
