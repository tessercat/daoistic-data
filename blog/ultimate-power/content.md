# Ultimate Power

有物混成
先天地生
Something wholesome existed
before heaven and earth were born.
寂兮寥兮
獨立不改
周行而不殆
可以為天下母
吾不知其名
Still and silent,
eternal and timeless,
moving in endless cycles,
I think of it as the all-mother,
but I'm not sure what to call her.

字之曰道
強為之名曰大
When I write about her, she's 道,
and if it helps, I say she's important,
大曰逝
逝曰遠
遠曰反
but I also say that
her greatness recedes,
recedes and rests,
rests and returns.

故道大
天大
地大
王亦大
Since that's why 道 is great,
the universe is vast,
the earth is huge,
and kings are important too.
域中有四大
而王居其一焉
All four are important to the motherland,
but kings have a place in just one,
人法地
地法天
天法道
道法自然
and people are to the earth,
as earth is to the universe,
as the universe is to 道,
as 道 is to what is.
