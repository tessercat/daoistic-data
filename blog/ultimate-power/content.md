# Ultimate Power

有物混成
先天地生
Something wholesome existed
before heaven and earth were born.
寂兮寥兮
獨立不改
周行而不殆
Still and silent,
eternal and timeless,
moving in endless cycles,
可以為天下母
吾不知其名
we might serve the all-mother,
but we don't even know her name.

字之曰道
強為之名曰大
When I write about her, she's 道,
and when I praise her, she's glorious,
大曰逝
逝曰遠
遠曰反
glory that fades,
fades and vanishes,
vanishes and returns.

故道大
天大
地大
王亦大
So 道 is wonderful,
the heavens are majestic,
the earth is sacred,
and kings are great too.
域中有四大
而王居其一焉
All four are important to the motherland,
but kings have a place in just one,
人法地
地法天
天法道
道法自然
and human beings are to the earth
as the earth is to the heavens
as the heavens are to 道
as 道 is to what is.
