# Us and Them

It's a tiny town,
just a few neighbours.
小國寡民

They gave us modern technology
but we didn't want to use it.
So they killed some of us
but we didn't run away.
使有什伯之器而不用
使民重死而不遠徙

Even if we had ships and wagons,
there was nowhere to go.
Even if we raised an army,
that was no way to resist.
雖有舟輿無所乘之
雖有甲兵無所陳之

Then they made us pay taxes
and took it all for themselves.
使民復結繩而用之

But our food is still delicious,
our clothes are beautiful,
our homes are peaceful,
our ways are friendly.
甘其食
美其服
安其居
樂其俗

Other towns are within sight of each other.
Our chickens and dogs can hear one another.
Neighbours die of old age and aren't cruel or kind to each other.
鄰國相望
雞犬之聲相聞
民至老死不相往來
