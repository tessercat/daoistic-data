Top talent is like water.
The genius of water is that it benefits everything and doesn't compete.
It settles in places that everyone else avoids,
and that's why it's a metaphor for Dao.
上善若水
水善利萬物而不爭
處衆人之所惡
故幾於道

Good character is stable,
good thoughts are deep,
good intentions are kind,
good teachings are true,
good discipline is fair,
good effort is helpful,
good action is timely.
居善地
心善淵
與善仁
言善信
正善治
事善能
動善時

The fact that it doesn't compete
is the reason it never loses.
夫唯不爭
故無尤
