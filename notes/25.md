Something existed
in perfect equilibrium
before the creation of heaven and earth.
Still and empty,
a changeless singularity,
circling, but flawless.
You might think of it as
ground state,
a map of the world,
an ideal world,
utopia.
有物混成
先天地生
寂兮寥兮
獨立不改
周行而不殆
可以為天下母

I don't know what to call it.
In a word, it's Dao.
If you ask me to describe it,
I'd say it's vast.
vast implies mortal,
mortal implies distance,
separate implies opposite.
吾不知其名
字之曰道
強為之名曰大
大曰逝
逝曰遠
遠曰反

It follows that
Dao is vast,
the universe is enormous,
the earth is huge,
and homage is important too.
There are four important things
in the middle realm,
and homage is but one.
故道大
天大
地大
王亦大
域中有四大而王居其一焉

Humanity is to the earth,
as the earth is to the universe,
as the the universe is to Dao,
as Dao is to what is.
人法地
地法天
天法道
道法自然
