There was something wholesome
before the creation of heaven and earth.
Still and welcoming,
ever-present and unchanging,
reaching everywhere, but unstressed,
think of it as everyone's mother.
有物混成
先天地生
寂兮寥兮
獨立不改
周行而不殆
可以為天下母

I don't know what to call it.
In a word, it's Dao.
If you ask me to describe it,
I'd say it's important,
and that important implies distant,
distant implies separate,
and separate implies reversal.
吾不知其名
字之曰道
強為之名曰大
大曰逝
逝曰遠
遠曰反

Since Dao is important,
the heavens are important,
the earth is important,
and kings are important too.
There are four important [things] in the middle realm,
and kings play a part in but one.
故道大
天大
地大
王亦大
域中有四大
而王居其一焉

Humanity is to the earth,
as earth is to the heavens,
as the heavens are to Dao,
as Dao is to what is.
人法地
地法天
天法道
道法自然
