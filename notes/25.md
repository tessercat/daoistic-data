Something wholesome existed
before heaven and earth were born.
Still and welcoming,
self-sufficient, unchanging,
spinning but undeviating,
I think of it as the all-mother,
but I don't give it a name.
有物混成
先天地生
寂兮寥兮
獨立不改
周行而不殆
可以為天下母
吾不知其名

When I write it down and call it Dao,
I name it, they call it great,
great, they call it distant,
distant, they call it separate,
separate, they call it revolution.
字之曰道
強為之名曰大
大曰逝
逝曰遠
遠曰反

So when Dao is great,
the heavens are great,
the earth is great,
and leaders are great too.
A homeland's greatness is four-fold,
but leaders embody their unity.
故道大
天大
地大
王亦大
域中有四大
而王居其一焉

Humanity is to the earth,
as earth is to the heavens,
as the heavens are to Dao,
as Dao is to what is.
人法地
地法天
天法道
道法自然
