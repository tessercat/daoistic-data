有物混成
先天地生
Something wholesome existed
before heaven and earth were born.
寂兮寥兮
獨立不改
周行而不殆
Still and silent,
eternal and timeless,
moving in endless cycles,
可以為天下母
吾不知其名
I might serve the all-mother,
but not in her name.

字之曰道
強為之名曰大
When I write about her, she's 道,
and when I invoke her, she's important.
大曰逝
逝曰遠
遠曰反
When she's important, she's deadly,
when she's deadly, she's distant,
when she's distant, she comes back.

故道大
天大
地大
王亦大
So 道 is important,
the heavens are important,
the earth is important,
and leadership is important too.
域中有四大
而王居其一焉
All four are important to us here on earth,
but leaders claim authority in just one.
人法地
地法天
天法道
道法自然
People are to the earth
as the earth is to the heavens
as the heavens are to 道
as 道 is to what is.

# Notes

I take this
as a refusal
to claim the authority
of 天 or 道
for oneself.

Authority is practical and worldly,
not principled or divine.
