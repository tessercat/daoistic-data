# Power

There was something whole and complete
before heaven and earth were born.
Still, silent,
eternal, timeless,
moving in endless cycles,
I think of it as the all-mother,
but I don't know its name.
有物混成
先天地生
寂兮寥兮
獨立不改
周行而不殆
可以為天下母
吾不知其名

They call it Dao
and they say it's great,
but greatness implies distance,
distance implies separation,
and separation implies rebellion.
字之曰道
強為之名曰大
大曰逝
逝曰遠
遠曰反

When Dao is great,
the heavens are great,
the earth is great,
and the king is great too.
Our homeland's greatness is four-fold,
but kingship embodies our common cause,
and humanity is to the earth,
as earth is to the heavens,
as the heavens are to Dao,
as Dao is to what is.
故道大
天大
地大
王亦大
域中有四大
而王居其一焉
人法地
地法天
天法道
道法自然
