Normal students hear about Dao and some are saved, some are lost.
Bad students hear about Dao and belittle it.

Enlightened Dao seems ignorant,
Progressive Dao seems backwards,
Balanced Dao seems stubborn.
明道若昧
進道若退
夷道若纇

The highest De seems empty,
the greatest candor seems dirty,
generous De seems stingy,
normal De seems lazy,
absolute truth seems fickle.
上德若谷
太白若辱
廣德若不足
建德若偷
質真若渝

It's a brilliant scholar with no publications,
an absolute genius that finishes late,
a booming voice that never speaks up,
a big idea with nothing to show.
大方無隅
大器晚成
大音希聲
大象無形

Dao is shy with no reputation,
but above all, Dao is good at both forgiving and reconciling.
