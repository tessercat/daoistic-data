# Natural Law

Nature creates us
and we follow its laws.
We're made of its matter
and we [] to its [].
道生之
德畜之
物形之
勢成之

From this,
not one of us
doesn't obey nature and repect its laws.
是以萬物莫不尊道而貴德

No one tells us
to obey nature
and respect its laws,
we just do.
道之尊
德之貴
夫莫之命
常自然

It follows that
nature creates us
and its laws constrain us,
lead us and educate us,
select us and cull us,
nourish us and cover us,
to create but not own,
to act but not insist,
to lead but not abuse.
故道生之
德畜之
長之育之
亭之毒之
養之覆之
生而不有
為而不恃
長而不宰

It's still the strange case
of doing the right thing.
是謂玄德
