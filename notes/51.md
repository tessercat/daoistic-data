Dao creates us,
De shepherds us,
circumstance forms us,
nurture matures us.
道生之
德畜之
物形之
勢成之

That's why of every creature,
none don't revere Dao or respect De.
是以萬物莫不尊道而貴德

No one commands us
to revere Dao
or respect De,
it's just always been like that.
道之尊
德之貴
夫莫之命常自然

Dao creates us,
De shepherds us,
leads us, educates us,
builds us up, tears us down,
supports us, lets us down.
故道生之
德畜之
長之育之
亭之毒之
養之覆之

Create but don't own it,
act but don't force it,
lead but don't control it.
生而不有
為而不恃
長而不宰

It's another strange case
of doing the right thing.
是謂玄德
