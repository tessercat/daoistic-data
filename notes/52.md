Society exists for a reason.
Think of it as society's mother.
天下有始
以為天下母

Once you've grasped its mother,
you'll understand her children.
既得其母
以知其子

Once you understand her children,
go protect them from the mother
close life unswerving.
既知其子
復守其母
沒身不殆

Shutter the windows,
bar the gate,
end life untested.
塞其兌
閉其門
終身不勤

Open the shutters,
support the cause,
end life unsaved.
開其兌
濟其事
終身不救

Notice the assholes, that's evidence,
protect the weak, that's strength.
見小曰明
守柔曰強

Shine this light,
go over the evidence
there's no neglect life complete.
用其光
復歸其明
無遺身殃
