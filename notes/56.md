Understanding it means not talking about it,
talking about it means not understanding it.
知者不言
言者不知

Close the shutters,
bar the gate,
dim the light,
stir the ash,
dull the edge,
loosen the knot.
塞其兑
閉其門
挫其銳
解其分
和其光
同其塵

That's called Xuantong, deep ambivalence,
是謂玄同

because it's impossible to grasp and still care,
and it's impossible to grasp and still forget,
故不可得而親
不可得而踈

It's impossible to grasp and get ahead,
and it's impossible to grasp and get hurt,
不可得而利
不可得而害

It's impossible to grasp and get respect
and it's impossible to grasp and hate
不可得而貴
不可得而賤

It's why we run the world well.
故為天下貴
