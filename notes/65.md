# 

In the old days, good managers
didn't explain anything to people.
They led by listening instead.
古之善為道者
非以明民
將以愚之

Teams are hard to control
when you keep a tight reign,
so it follows that
micro-managing the company
is bad for the company,
and not micro-managing is good.
民之難治以其智多
故以智治國國之賊
不以智治國國之福

Managers that recognize this
are just facing facts.
知此兩者亦𥡴式

Constantly reminding yourself to face facts
is Xuande, the secret of doing the right thing.
常知𥡴式
是謂玄德

When Xuande is ingrained,
pervasive,
gives them free reign,
that's when the team is well in hand.
玄德深矣
遠矣
與物反矣
然後乃至大順

# Notes

This poem
pits 明 vs 愚
and 智 vs 知
in the context of
how old-school Dharma bums 為道者
regard citizens 民
when governing a country 治國.

𥡴式 is a rabbit hole
of magic and divination,
but I think of it as
*looking for clues*,
or *examining the evidence*
to solve the mystery 玄
of doing the right thing 德.

I like
深, 遠 and 與物反
as solutions to
玄德's moral sudoku of
生而不有
為而不恃
長而不宰.
