# Salvation

天下皆謂我道大
似不肖
Everyone says that my Dao is important
and doesn't seem to fade.
夫唯大故似不肖
In fact,
it doesn't seem to fade
because it's so important.
若肖久矣
I think it's been fading for a long time.
其細也夫
It's tiny, isn't it?

我有三寶
持而保之
I have three treasures
that I cherish and protect.
一曰慈
二曰儉
三曰不敢為天下先
The first is mercy,
the second is caution,
and the third is fear of taking the initiative.
慈故能勇
儉故能廣
不敢為天下先故能成器長
Compassion makes us heroes,
caution makes us generous,
and refusing to strike first lets us build lasting peace.

今
舍慈且勇
舍儉且廣
舍後且先
死矣
The modern world is
too angry for compassion,
too spoiled for caution,
too proud for restraint,
and it gets us killed.
夫慈以戰則勝
以守則固
Mercy in attack is the victor,
in defense, keeps us safe.
天將救之
以慈衛之
The gods favour us
when pity protects us.


## Notes

https://ctext.org/dao-de-jing#n11658

This looks to me
like an argument
that leverages an old saying
to counsel against attack
and promote defense
in times of war.

Laozi turns positive virtues into deadly sins
when they're un-tempered by the less sexy but perennial virtues
the modern world sets aside (今舍):
compassion (慈),
economy (儉),
caution (不敢)
and humility (後).
