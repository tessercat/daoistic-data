# 

Peasants starve when leaders impose heavy taxes. That's when we go hungry.
Workers get upset when leaders interfere. That's what makes governing so hard.
Citizens risk death for a better life. That's when we risk our lives.
民之飢以其上食稅之多是以飢
民之難治以其上之有為是以難治
民之輕死以其求生之厚是以輕死

Only those [without the means to control life]
are truly capable of caring for life.
夫唯無以生為者
是賢於貴生
